HOUSTON GRABS HEAD OF MUSIC STAFF FROM LA OPERA

Houston Grand Opera has hired the Canadian Miah Im as music director of its (young) artists studio.
She has been head of music staff at Los Angeles Opera for the past three years. ‘I will bring my passion and experience in support of this wonderful artform and help to inspire the young artists on their journey to becoming world class singers,’ she said.
